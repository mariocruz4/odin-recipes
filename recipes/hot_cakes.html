Hot Cakes 101

Delicious Hot Cakes in autum

This is Hot Cakes 101, it's a very simple recipe that allows you to make delicious and floffy hot cakes.

It's a recipe for aproximately 20 - 25 hot cakes.

If you want less only change the elements mark with a *

Ingredients

    Dry Elements

  • 3 Cups of All purpose fluor*
  • 1/4 Cup of Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp of Baking Powder
  • 1 Tsp of Salt
  • 1 Tsp of Baking Soda

    • Wet Elements

  • 2 1/2 Cups of Milk* (Lactose Free or Regular)
  • 1/4 Cup of Apple Vinager
  • 1/4 Cup of Vegteble Oil
  • 1 Tbsp of Vanilla Extract
  • 3 Eggs*

    • The Cooking

  • Salted Butter

Steps

    The Dough

  1. Mix all the Dry Elements in a Bowl (Make sure they're all well mixed)
  2. Mix all the Wet Elementes in another recipient (If you make some foam it's perfect)
  3. Mix the Dry and Wet Elements gently with a wooden shovel or a large spoon. (Do no stirr)You want to see some bubbles.
  4. Let rest the dough for 10 ~ 15 min.

    5. The Cooking

  5. Heat a nonstick pan with a medium fire.
  6. Add a good and nice portion of salted butter and spread it all over the pan
  7. Turn the fire to low
  8. With a large spoon pour some dough in the pan (It doesn't matter if it's not a perfect circle, you're not IHOP)
  9. Please, please, please! Do not attemp to squeeze them.
  10. Wait until the bubbles on the top side appear all over the hot cake
  11. When the bubbles no longer closes, flipp the hot cake
  12. DO NOT SEQUEEZE THEM!!
  13. Wait 3 ~ 5 minutes and your hot cakes is ready to serve

    14. Plating

  14. Serve the hot cakes with the fruit and topics of your choice. I personel recommed with maple sirup or condensed milk
