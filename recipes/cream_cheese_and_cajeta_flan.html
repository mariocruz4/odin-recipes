Cream cheese and cajeta sauce flan

A slice of flan.

For those who doesn't know what cajeta means, here it is:

Cajeta is a traditional Mexican candy, made mainly with goat's milk, which is characterized by being thick.

The history of the flan seems to be with Rome, and, essencialy is a dessert with sugar, milk and many egg yolks.

Nowdays it's a typical Mexican dessert, so common that I don't know anyone who doesn't like it.

But this time we're goona make it with cream cheese, like Philadelphia and cajeta.

Ingredients

  • 5 Eggs
  • 250gr. of cream cheese
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of milk or 1 can of evaporated milk
  • 1 Tsp of vanilla
  • 1/4 cup of Cajeta
